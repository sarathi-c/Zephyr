EVENT 1

Panel discussion on emerging trends

In this event, a panel of experts from academia and industry will come together to discuss the latest advancements and emerging trends in the field of electronics and communications engineering. The panel will explore topics such as 5G communication, artificial intelligence, and the internet of things (IoT), and discuss the impact these technologies are likely to have on the industry in the coming years. Participants will have the opportunity to engage with the panelists and ask questions about the latest research, technology, and applications in the field.

Place: Mini Seminar Hall

Time: 10am-12pm
