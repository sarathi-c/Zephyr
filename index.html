ZEPHYR 2023

Organized by Electronics and Communication Department
of Rajalakshmi Engineering College

Welcome to ZEPHYR the annual technical symposium of the Electronics and Communication department of Rajalakshmi Engineering College

ABOUT

The Zephyr 2023 Annual symposium of the Electronics and Communication Department of Rajalakshmi Engineering College will take place on May 21st, 2023, at our campus in Chennai. This year's event promises to be an exciting and informative one, with a wide range of activities planned for students, faculty, and industry professionals. One of the highlights of Zephyr 2023 will be the recognition of outstanding individuals in the field of electronics and communication. Awards will be given out to researchers who have made significant contributions to the field, as well as students who have excelled in their academic and research endeavors. These awards not only recognize the hard work and dedication of these individuals but also serve as inspiration for future generations of students and researchers.

In addition to the awards ceremony, Zephyr 2023 will feature a variety of technical workshops, keynote speeches, and competitions designed to challenge and engage participants. These activities will cover a wide range of topics, including emerging technologies, telecommunications, signal processing, and more.

EVENTS

1)Panel discussion on emerging trends

2)Workshop on embedded systems design

3)Paper presentation for students

We hope that these events will provide a valuable opportunity for participants to engage with the latest research and trends in electronics and communications engineering, gain practical experience in important areas of the field, and connect with experts and peers in the industry.

We encourage attendees to follow us on social media to stay updated on the latest symposium news and announcements.
