EVENT 3

Paper presentation for students

In this event, students will have the opportunity to showcase their research work in the field of electronics and communications engineering. Participants will create posters that summarize their research findings and present their work to other participants. This event will be a great way for students to gain experience presenting their research work and receive feedback from their peers and experts in the field. All symposium attendees will have the opportunity to view the posters and engage with the student presenters to learn more about their research.

Place: TIFAC Hall

Time: 4am-5pm
