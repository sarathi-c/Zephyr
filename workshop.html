EVENT 2

Workshop on embedded systems design

This hands-on workshop will provide participants with the opportunity to learn about embedded systems design. Participants will learn how to design and program microcontrollers, work with sensors and actuators, and build simple embedded systems. This workshop will be suitable for both beginners and more experienced participants, and will provide a valuable opportunity to gain practical experience in this important area of electronics and communications engineering.

Place: Auditorium

Time: 2pm-4pm
